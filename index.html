To apply to Far Eastern Federal University (FEFU) as a foreign student, you will typically need to provide academic transcripts, proof of language proficiency (such as TOEFL or IELTS scores), and a passport. Additionally, you may need to obtain a student visa for the duration of your studies. FEFU offers a range of support services for international students, including language courses and cultural integration programs to ensure a smooth transition into university life. With its diverse academic programs and strong research opportunities, FEFU provides an enriching and supportive environment for international students to pursue their education.
